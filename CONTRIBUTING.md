# Contributing to NoLag Launcher

Thanks for your interest in improving NoLag Launcher!

## License & ownership

- The Project is offered to the public under the **GNU AGPL v3.0** (see [LICENSE](LICENSE)
  and [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md)).
- NoLag Launcher is **single-owner**. Before any contribution can be merged you must
  agree to the **[Contributor License Agreement](CLA.md)**. It assigns the rights in your
  contribution to the Owner, so the Project stays under one owner who can maintain and —
  if they choose — relicense or sell commercial licenses for it. You keep the right to
  use your own contribution (CLA §7), but you do not own the Project.

## Agreeing to the CLA

When you open your first pull request, the **CLA bot** will comment and ask you to sign.
Sign by posting this comment, **exactly**, in the pull request:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

The bot records your signature in `signatures/version1/cla.json`. Your pull request
cannot be merged until the CLA is signed.

## Submitting changes

1. Fork the repo and create a branch from `master`.
2. Keep pull requests focused and follow the existing code style.
3. Make sure the project builds (`./gradlew :app:assembleDebug`) before opening a PR.
4. Keep the `SPDX-License-Identifier` headers intact in any files you touch, and add them
   to new source files.
5. Describe what changed and why.

By submitting a contribution you confirm it is your original work and that you have the
right to submit it under the CLA.
